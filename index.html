About Us

At 360 Tinted, we provide expert ceramic and carbon window tinting for all types of vehicles – especially Teslas – in San Carlos, Redwood City, San Mateo, Palo Alto, and surrounding cities. We use premium materials and professional techniques to protect your car and improve its style and comfort.

Our Top Services

Discover the perfect tinting solution for your vehicle. We offer premium films for ultimate protection and style.

Carbon Tinting (Doors & Rear)

Enhance your ride with high-performance film that blocks heat and UV rays, offering a clean, stylish look without signal interference.

Ceramic Tinting (Full Car)

Experience superior heat rejection and enhanced UV protection with our premium ceramic film for unmatched comfort and clarity.

Custom Large Vehicle Tint

Tailored solutions for SUVs, vans, and vehicles with panoramic roofs. Get the perfect film for your unique vehicle needs.
